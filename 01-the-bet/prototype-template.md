# The Prototype Bet

> **Purpose.** A prototype is the cheapest way to make a bet legible. Numbers and decks let people nod politely; a working artefact forces a reaction. The job of this section is to record *what was built, why, and how we will know if it was the wrong idea*.
>
> **How to use it.** Fill in after the prototype is shareable. Be honest about what it does and does not show — a prototype that overclaims is worse than one that underclaims.

## Kill criteria
*The evidence that would make us stop. A bet without kill criteria is a hope. Examples: "if fewer than X% of testers complete the agentic flow without help"; "if the dominant feedback is 'why not just use ChatGPT'."*
